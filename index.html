About
The Western Drone Regionals is a national collegiate drone racing championship that will be held at the University of California, Berkeley in February 2018. Join us to compete with teams from across the nation, explore the San Francisco Bay Area and attend a personal Career Fair with some of the best companies in the drone industry and Silicon Valley.
